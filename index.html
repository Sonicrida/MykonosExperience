Why Mykonos

Do you like gorgeous seaside views? Maybe you love great food(who doesn't?). Or maybe you just want to get away from everything. Even if you none of those fit you, Mykonos is still the place for you.

Where is Mykonos?

Mykonos is smack dab in the middle of the ocean off the shores of Greece. It's surrounded by tons of other islands as well but those don't matter too much since you'll never want to leave anyway.

I'm hungry!

If you're hungry, there's plenty of places to grab something to eat! The best part is that almost everything has a waterfront view so you can enjoy the ocean while you eat.
